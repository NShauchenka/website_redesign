Our Galleries

Main Gallery
Our versatile main gallery can accomodate 125 people standing for cocktails, 100 people for a buffet or 60 for a sit-down dinner.
John Greenwald Gallery
This space will comfortably accomodate 20 people for a meeting or a more intimate sit-down dinner.
The Workspace
For meetings, workshops or gatherings of up to 12 people. A perfect space for staging and preparation of food and drinks for a larger event.

ALL's lively arts center and galleries are centrally located in the Gates Block building in downtown Lowell. The galleries in this classic brick building, once home to manufacturing and retail businesses, have been transformed with beautiful hardwood floors, gallery lighting and plenty of natural light from large windows.

The galleries are always filled with ever-changing exhibits of local and regional art.

Our three distinct rooms can be combined to provide the space that you need to host meetings, parties, bridal showers, classes, receptions, lectures and more. Renting one or all three rooms will surely provide a uniquely creative venue for your next function.
